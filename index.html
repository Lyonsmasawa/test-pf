Trans~Fuse

The application centralizes all blood banks in all Kenyan hospitals. A patient/relative installs the app and searches for the required blood type and locates it in a specific hospital in a certain region. He/she contacts the hospital blood bank for delivery or reservation.

The application also aims at partnering with ambulance services for blood delivery. Moreso,the app has a donation button where donation drives are scheduled so as to ensure that blood is always available.Lastly,there is a section where donor communicates directly with the recipient.

Our Company

Welcome to IdeaLabsAfrica, a company dedicated to bringing innovative solutions to the world. We are passionate about improving people's lives through technology. With our three cutting-edge products - a health app, a tourism app, and a climate action app - we aim to make a positive impact in various sectors. Our health app focuses on providing personalized health and wellness recommendations, while our tourism app aims to enhance the travel experience by offering curated itineraries and local insights. Additionally, our climate action app empowers individuals and organizations to take meaningful steps towards a more sustainable future. At IdeaLabsAfrica, we believe in the power of technology to transform lives and create a better world.
